Privacy Policy

AMGMastermindBot - Your Ultimate Telegram Quiz Bot with a Taylor Swift Twist

Welcome to Our Mastermind Universe

Inspired by Taylor Swift's *Mastermind*, this Privacy Policy outlines how AMGMastermindBot collects, uses, and safeguards your data while you enjoy our Telegram quiz bot. We’re committed to protecting your privacy with the same precision Taylor uses to craft her iconic Easter eggs—because your trust is everything to us.

Data Collection in Groups

When AMGMastermindBot joins your Telegram group, it gathers the following details to ensure seamless functionality:

  • The group’s title and unique ChatID, as provided by Telegram.
  • The date of the last message sent in the group to keep track of activity.
  • A list of members who have interacted with the bot by sending at least one message in the group.
  • Custom settings applied by group admins using the /settings command.

All data is tied to the group’s ChatID and used exclusively to enhance your quiz experience—think of it as our way of setting the stage for your *Mastermind* moment.

Your Interactions with the Bot

When you engage with AMGMastermindBot in a group or private chat, we collect and update the following:

  • Your first and last name, as provided on Telegram.
  • Your username, if you’ve set one.
  • The date of your most recent interaction with the bot.

This information is linked to your unique UserID from Telegram, helping us personalize your experience—like curating the perfect playlist for your quiz journey.

Secondary Information

We don’t store secondary details like profile pictures or admin lists. Instead, these are fetched in real-time from Telegram’s servers when needed (e.g., when you use the /profile command). It’s all about keeping things light and secure—like keeping the spotlight on the quiz, not your data.

Access by Bot Administrators

Our founders and admins access logs only for maintenance and support purposes. They may view:

  • Logs for new bot instance creation.
  • Requests to reset group or user data.
  • Donation-related activities.

If a user or group is blocked for violating our rules, admins may access the last 200 messages to resolve appeals or disputes. This access is limited and strictly for ensuring fairness—like Taylor ensuring every lyric hits just right.

Game Image Generation

AMGMastermindBot creates game images on our own servers, ensuring no data is shared with external services. Your privacy stays locked down tighter than the secrets in Taylor’s vault tracks.
